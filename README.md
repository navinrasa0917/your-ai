# Your-AI
## ***Your-AI Overview***

***Live demo*** 
   https://your-ai-seven.vercel.app

***Your-AI*** is an assistive, human-centric application designed to empower elderly people and individuals with disabilities by providing easy access to government schemes, employment opportunities, and para-sports guidance. The application aims to bridge the information gap faced by marginalized communities by offering a simple, accessible, and inclusive digital platform.

### ***Government Scheme Assistance***

One of the key features of ***Your-AI*** is helping users discover government schemes available to them based on their disability type, age, and category. The application explains scheme benefits and eligibility in a user-friendly manner and directly redirects users to official government websites to apply, ensuring transparency, accuracy, and safety.

### ***Job Matching for Disabled Individuals***

***Your-AI*** supports employment inclusion by helping people with disabilities find suitable job opportunities according to their skills and abilities. The application recommends jobs that match user profiles and guides them through the application process, promoting self-reliance and financial independence.

### ***Para-Olympics Support Section***

The application includes a dedicated Para-Olympics section for differently-abled athletes. This section provides guidance on para-sports, eligibility criteria, and participation opportunities. Users are assisted step-by-step and redirected to official para-olympics application portals for registration.

### ***Multilingual & Accessibility Features***

To ensure accessibility for a diverse population, **Your-AI** supports three languages:

***English***

***Tamil***

***Hindi***

***sign language translator***

*A special feature of the application is a sign-language translator, which converts sign language into text and voice output. This feature helps users with hearing or speech impairments communicate more effectively.*

### ***Help & Learning Resources***

***Your-AI*** includes a Help & Support section where users can contact the team by sending messages for assistance. The platform also provides video tutorials to guide users on how to use the application, apply for schemes, and access services easily.

### ***Conclusion***

***Your-AI*** leverages technology and artificial intelligence to create an inclusive ecosystem that supports elderly people and individuals with disabilities, enabling them to access opportunities, resources, and guidance with dignity and ease.
